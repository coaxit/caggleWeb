Quotation Search

Caggle and our lenders can use quotation searches, sometimes called soft searches, to work out whether you're eligible or pre-approved for a particular product. These searches do not affect your credit score.

Quotation searches are different from credit application searches, which happen when you actually apply for a product. Too many credit application searches in a short space of time can damage your credit score. That's why it's best to use quotation searches while you're shopping around for a product, so you know whether you're eligible to apply. Caggle helps to protect your score by using quotation searches to take the guesswork out of applying for credit.

By clicking on 'Match me', you're giving us consent to share your data with our lenders so they can run a soft search and an initial affordability assessment. Your data won't be sold to any lenders and they won't retain your data or use it for any other purpose.
